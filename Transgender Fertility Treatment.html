Nazar




Nazar introduced and perfected new fertility treatments that have led to impressive fertility success rates. The treatments include sperm donation, insemination (IUI), in vitro fertilization (IVF) for same sex female couples and egg donation and gestational surrogacy for gay couples and transgendered people. At Nazar we believe that “everyone should experience the gift of parenthood.”
